About Us – STUDIO 13 FAMILY SALON

Founded in 2014, STUDIO 13 FAMILY SALON has been a trusted destination for families in Navi Mumbai seeking premium hair, beauty, and grooming services. With years of experience, our dedicated team of skilled professionals combines creativity, expertise, and personalized care to deliver exceptional results for men, women, and children alike.

We proudly operate two branches in Navi Mumbai – Sanpada and Nerul – each designed to offer a comfortable, modern, and hygienic environment where every client can relax and enjoy a luxurious salon experience.

At STUDIO 13, we believe that every client deserves not only a stylish look but also a personalized and enjoyable salon experience. We use premium products from world-renowned brands like L’Oréal and Kérastase, ensuring your hair and skin receive the best care possible. Our partnerships with these brands reflect our commitment to quality and excellence in every service we offer.

From trendy haircuts and hair coloring to professional bridal services and beauty treatments, we tailor every service to suit your unique style and needs. Our mission is to enhance confidence, style, and well-being for every family member who walks through our doors.

Experience the difference at STUDIO 13 FAMILY SALON – where style meets care, and every visit is a celebration of beauty and confidence.
