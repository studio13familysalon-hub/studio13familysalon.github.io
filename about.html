About Us

Welcome to STUDIO 13 FAMILY SALON

At STUDIO 13 FAMILY SALON, we are more than just a salon—we are a family-oriented haven where beauty meets comfort. Established in 2015, our salon has been serving the vibrant community of Mumbai, India, with top-notch hair, beauty, and grooming services. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming environment for everyone, from children to adults, ensuring every visit is a delightful experience.

Our team of skilled professionals brings years of expertise in modern styling techniques, using high-quality, eco-friendly products to deliver exceptional results. Whether you're looking for a fresh haircut, a rejuvenating facial, or bridal makeup, we tailor our services to your unique needs.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality beauty and grooming services in a friendly, hygienic environment, empowering our clients to look and feel their best every day.

Our Vision

To be the leading family salon in the region, known for innovation, customer satisfaction, and community involvement.

What Sets Us Apart

We specialize in a wide range of services, including haircuts, coloring, facials, waxing, makeup, and more. Our commitment to hygiene, personalized care, and the latest trends sets us apart. We use eco-friendly products and stay updated with industry advancements to ensure the best for our clients.

  • Experienced and certified stylists
  • Family-friendly atmosphere
  • Affordable pricing with quality
  • Eco-friendly and safe products
  • Online booking and consultations
  • Awards for excellence in service
