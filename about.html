About Us

Who We Are

STUDIO 13 FAMILY SALON is a premium family salon dedicated to delivering professional hair, beauty, and grooming services for men, women, and children. Since our establishment in 2015, we have built a reputation for quality, hygiene, and personalized care.

Our salon blends modern styling techniques with a warm, family-friendly atmosphere—making every visit comfortable, relaxing, and rewarding.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable salon services in a hygienic and welcoming environment while ensuring every client feels confident and valued.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted family salon brand in Navi Mumbai, known for excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
